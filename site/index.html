How it works

Three steps to a focused day.

1

Plan your day

Add the tasks you need to complete. Estimate how long each will take. Arrange them in order.

2

Start your session

Hit start and Drifter begins counting down your first task. The widget appears on your screen.

3

Stay on track

The widget shows time remaining at a glance. When a task ends, the next one begins automatically.

The Widget

Always visible.
Never in the way.

A compact floating panel that sits on top of all your windows. Drag it anywhere. Shows exactly what you need: what you're working on, how much time you have left, and what's coming next. No tab-switching, no checking the clock.
